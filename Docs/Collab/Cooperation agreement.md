## Purpose of the Collaboration
The purpose of this collaboration is to complete the 4rd semester project through structured teamwork, clear communication, and shared responsibility.

---

## Ambition Level
We aim to create a solid and well-developed product accompanied by an in-depth, well-written, and well-structured report.

---

## Scope and Responsibility
Each member is responsible for completing their assigned tasks and actively contributing to the overall progress of the project.

- All members must take responsibility for their individual tasks and ensure they are completed correctly.
- If a member experiences difficulties, they must ask the group for help.
- If the issue cannot be resolved internally, assistance should be requested from the project supervisor.

All work must be reviewed by **at least one group member** who did not participate in creating it.  
The reviewer becomes the contact person for the task if issues arise later.

## Group Tone
We keep a casual and respectful tone and communicate as we do in everyday life.

- All feedback must be constructive and respectful
- There are no stupid questions
- Asking for help is encouraged
- Personal attacks are not tolerated

## Communication

## Sprint Structure

- Sprint length: **2 week**
- Sprint planning takes place during (day) meetings
- Each sprint includes:
  - Defined tasks
  - A responsible person per task
  - A reviewer per task
- Sprint review happens at the next scrum meeting

---

## Time Schedule & Work Expectations
- Members are expected to actively contribute throughout the week
- Tasks must be updated on the GitHub task board
- Delays must be communicated **as early as possible**

---

## Absence
- Planned absence must be communicated **no later than the day before**
- Acute illness or unforeseen events must be communicated as soon as possible
- Repeated unexplained absence is considered a contract breach

---

## Decision-Making Process
- Decisions are made by **consensus**
- If consensus cannot be reached, a vote is held
- Majority decides
- Only members present (physically or online) have voting rights
- Important decisions may be voted on via Discord if physical presence is not possible and a valid reason is given in advance

---

## Disagreements
- Conflicts are handled internally through dialogue
- If unresolved, meeting would be made to take the issue
- If necessary, the supervisor is involved

---

## Consequences for Contract Breach

### Failure to Attend Meetings
1. First offense: Verbal reminder  
2. Second offense: Written warning in meeting minutes  
3. Third offense: Escalation to group meeting  
4. Continued issues: Supervisor contacted  

---

### Failure to Complete Tasks
- Missed deadlines without notice must be explained to the group
- Repeated failure results in:
  - Loss of task priority
  - Redistribution of tasks
  - Possible escalation to supervisor
